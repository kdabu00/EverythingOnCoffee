Brewing Coffee

Make sure that your tools are thoroughly cleaned after each use! Rinse the equipment with clear, hot water and dry with an absorbent towel while making sure for no leftover grinds or build up of coffee oil which can make the future cups taste bitter. When brewing make sure you do the golden ratio of one or two tablespoons of ground coffee to every 6 ounces of water(adjust to taste). The amount of time that the water is in contact with the coffee grounds is very important for flavor, for a drip the contact time should be ~5 minutes, for French Press contact time should be 2-4minutes. Espresso is very quick with 20-30s contact time, and cold brew should steep for about 12 hours.
